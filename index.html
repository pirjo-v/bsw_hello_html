Spring in Paris

Monday, May 7

On your first evening in Paris, make sure to

  • Walk the Champs-Élysées from Arc de Triomphe to Louvre
  • Eat all the bread (it's not going to be the last time)
  • Drink some red wine
  • Look at the lights of Eiffel Tower from a distance

Tuesday, May 8

Tuesday was a sunny day and we took full advantage of it by having a picnic at the Eiffel Tower. We took inspiration from this Fat Tire Tours video.

On a sunny day in Paris make sure to

  1. Drink coffee, preferrably in a sidewalk cafe
  2. Visit Eiffel Tower and have a picnic at Champ de Mars
  3. Eat all the bread, again
  4. Walk along Seine river or up to Sacre-Coeur in Montmartre
